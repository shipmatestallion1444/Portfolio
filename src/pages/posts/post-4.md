Terry Pratchett's Discworld series is a beloved collection of fantasy novels that have captivated readers for decades. Set on a flat, disc-shaped world supported by four elephants standing on the back of a giant turtle, Discworld offers a unique blend of humor, satire, and imaginative storytelling.
